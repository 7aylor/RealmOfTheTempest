About Bramble Gate

Bramble Gate Studios started as an alias for the development of Realm of the Tempest, which is a game that I created to help cope with chronic pain. It's meant to represent perseverance through suffering, and has become a symbol of hope.

To learn more about Realm of the Tempest, click here.

Bramble Gate Academy

A dream that I hope to realize one day is to create free educational material about the topics I have grown to love through game development. As a way to give back to the community, I'd be honored to share what I've learned about high level math, theoretical computer science, art and color theory, and mental health. Realm of the Tempest is an archive of lessons I've learned, and revisit regularly for personal growth, and that is a process I'd like to share.

If you'd like to support this ambition, you can donate to us through Patreon. You can also join the community on Discord.
